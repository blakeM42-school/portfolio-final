Blake Mangola

Goal:

My goal is to motivate genuine interest across multiple domains for the benefit of the viewer. I hope to encourage others be the best they can be both in the classroom and in society. With an emphasis on Computer Science, I strive to provide the the clearest explanations of difficult concepts and processes. I hope to create follow along tutorials that benefit struggling students by teaching in unconventional ways, as if your friend was explaining to you how to do something in the simplest way possible. I place a high priority on sharing exclusive tips that can be regarded as trade secrets, prioritizing the viewer and ensuring the lessons are genuine and relatable.
